Creatively Fueled. Competitively Motivated

I'm the dude who's got some games to play. I get immersed in game worlds and just have fun playing around with the systems and exploring what could be done in games. Games require a lot of time, effort, and love to make, as I hope to publish a game somehow in the future.

As a game design major, I hope to utilize the skills learned in my major to be a great asset for your company. I have programming experience to read through different languages, see what makes them tick, and then modify or even make various different programs. I'm big into agile development as I rapidly Iterate with my programs and version control them.

My main experience though is art experience. I freelance and make characters for people online amongst participating in other art projects as well. I know how to draw and to draft out designs, I know how to layout a graphic scene to effectively communicate with the customer or end user, and lastly, I know how to make a finished art piece for everyone to enjoy under a strict deadline. I love being artistic as I try to find new, innovative ways to become a better artist.

I Love to work in teams of people as I have lots of experience with group projects over the school year. I communicate with my team-members often as we break down a complicated task one step at a time and go from there
