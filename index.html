David Enersen

Graphic Designer | Game Designer


On August 13 of 1993 I was brought forth to this world. And almost immediately as a baby I was exposed to video games. I was immersed, and wowed by what games could do. And they can do a lot of things, from bringing you into a world filled with excitement and adventures to even discovering who I am as a person. I have collected so much games over the years, and each of those games brought about many different types of fun on the table. That’s why I want to make an impact in the gaming industry. Gaming has such an influence in my life

I LOVE being social with my friends, or getting myself involved with other hobbies or misadventures. Drawing in particular has been a passion of mine, and it started by accident when one of my friends was asking for feedback on an art tutorial. Ever since then I’ve been passionate about designing characters, trying to convey vast and different personalities with different designs.
